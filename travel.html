Location Details

Castle Green is an historic landmark in the downtown area of Pasadena, known as Old Pas. There are many restaurants and shops in the area, and you can find a directory map here.

Castle Green
99 S. Raymond Ave.
Pasadena, CA 91105

Getting to the Ceremony

On the day of the wedding, we will provide a shuttle between the Westin Pasadena and Castle Green.

For guests who are local or have rented a car, there are two nearby parking structures with a $6 maximum fee. Del Mar Station Parking Garage is on the corner of Del Mar and Raymond. The Schoolhouse Parking Garage is on the corner of Raymond and Green. There is also metered parking along Raymond and timed parking along Green.

For Out-of-Town Guests

Lodging

We have set aside a block of rooms at the Westin Pasadena for Friday, June 20 and Saturday, June 21 . You may make a reservation online at the hotel page for our wedding. You may also make reservations by calling the Reservations Department directly at 866-837-4181 and identifying yourself as part of the Kimberly Boddy and Timothy Hill wedding block. The cut-off date for accepting reservations into this block is Wednesday, May 21, 2014.

Check-in time is 3 p.m. Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours in advance. The group rate for a single/double occupancy room is $119.00 (+ 15.09% tax). The charge for each additional adult is $15. Roll-away beds are available for the single occupancy rooms at a rate of $25 per night.

If you prefer a different location, other nearby hotels are listed below. Please be aware that the shuttle to and from Castle Green will pick up and drop off only at the Westin.

  • Sheraton Pasadena Hotel
  • Hilton Pasadena
  • Pasadena Inn

Airports

Transportation

The Westin Pasadena provides driving directions to the hotel. They also provide information about transportation options in Pasadena. If you would like to use public transit, the Pasadena Department of Transportation has more information, including getting from LAX to Pasadena.
